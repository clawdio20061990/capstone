## Slide 2: The Problem

**Visual:** Split image — left: cat alone at home looking bored, right: guilty owner at work

**Text:**
```
Indoor Cats Are Bored & Depressed

• 127M+ cats in EU + UK, millions spend 8-10 hours alone daily (FEDIAF, 2024)
• ~50% show signs of boredom or depression (Ellis et al., 2013; Casey & Bradshaw, 2008)
• Owners feel guilty but lack time to play
• Existing toys: random, repetitive, cats lose interest in days

The Result: Sedentary cats, anxious owners, growing vet bills
```

**Speaker Notes:** "Studies show that approximately half of indoor cats exhibit signs of boredom or depression. Urban professionals — our target market — work 8-10 hour days. They love their cats, they feel guilty, but they simply don't have time. Current solutions don't work: random laser toys bore cats within days, manual play requires being home, and traditional toys sit unused."

*Sources: Ellis, S.L.H., et al. (2013). "AAFP and ISFM Feline Environmental Needs Guidelines," Journal of Feline Medicine & Surgery; Casey, R.A. & Bradshaw, J.W.S. (2008). "The effects of additional socialisation for kittens in a rescue centre on their behaviour," Applied Animal Behaviour Science; FEDIAF (2024) European Pet Food Industry Annual Report.*

## Slide 5: How It Works

**Visual:** Diagram showing: Cat → Camera Detection → AI Processing → Adaptive Laser Movement → Cloud Learning

**Text:**
```
Edge AI + Cloud Learning

1. Computer vision detects cat position
2. AI analyzes engagement in real-time
3. Laser patterns adapt to cat's response
4. Session data sent to cloud for retraining
5. Improved AI model deployed overnight

Each cat gets a personalized AI model.
The more they play, the better it gets.
```

**Speaker Notes:** "Here's the magic: we run computer vision on the device itself — edge AI. This means low latency, no video sent to the cloud, and instant response. But after each session, we send gameplay data — not video, just position and engagement metrics — to our cloud platform. That's where the real learning happens. Every night, we retrain the model for your specific cat. Within weeks, Reactacat knows your cat better than any toy ever could."

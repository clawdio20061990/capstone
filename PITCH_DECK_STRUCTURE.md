## Slide 5: How It Works

**Visual:** Diagram showing: Cat → Camera Detection → AI Processing → Adaptive Laser Movement → Cloud Learning

**Text:**
```
Edge AI + Cloud Learning

1. Computer vision detects cat position
2. AI analyzes engagement in real-time
3. Laser patterns adapt to cat's response
4. Session data sent to cloud for retraining
5. Improved AI model deployed overnight

Each cat gets a personalized AI model.
The more they play, the better it gets.
```

**Speaker Notes:** "Here's the magic: we run computer vision on the device itself — edge AI. This means low latency, no video sent to the cloud, and instant response. But after each session, we send gameplay data — not video, just position and engagement metrics — to our cloud platform. That's where the real learning happens. Every night, we retrain the model for your specific cat. Within weeks, Reactacat knows your cat better than any toy ever could."

## Slide 7: Business Model

**Visual:** Hardware + Subscription icon, revenue breakdown pie chart

**Text:**
```
Hardware + SaaS Hybrid Model

Hardware: €150 one-time
• €42-68 gross margin (Year 1-3)
• DTC distribution only (own the relationship)

Subscription: €3-8/month
• Standard (€3): Basic AI, monthly reports
• Premium (€6): Advanced modes, detailed analytics
• Ultra (€8): Multi-cat, veterinary insights

Target: 50% subscription conversion
LTV: €270 | CAC: €45 | LTV:CAC = 6:1
```

**Speaker Notes:** "We're not just selling hardware. The hardware is the entry point. The subscription is where the long-term value lives. €150 gets you the device. But the AI only keeps learning with a subscription. We've modeled three tiers. Our base case is 50% conversion — conservative for a product where the value is this clear. At 50%, lifetime value is €270, acquisition cost is €45. That's a 6-to-1 LTV to CAC ratio. Healthy unit economics from day one."

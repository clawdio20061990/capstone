## Business Research Focus

### Research Questions
All three core research questions are valid and included in business research:
1. **Is there a viable market for an AI-powered autonomous cat toy in the EU?**
2. **What business model (hardware + subscription) is most viable for Reactacat?**
3. **What regulatory, financial, and market conditions enable Reactacat's success?**

### Research Methodology
- **Quantitative:** Market size research, competitor pricing analysis, financial modeling
- **Qualitative:** Expert interviews (incorporated into user testing phase when Dmytro provides results)
- **Data sources:** Secondary research (industry reports, competitor analysis, market data)
- **No other survey methods planned** — limited resources

### Data Standards for MBA Context
- **Data must be optimistic but grounded in reality** — MBA business case projects balance aspiration with feasibility
- All assumptions clearly stated and justified
- Sources validated and cited
- If using best-case/optimistic scenarios, explicitly label them as such

### Known Risk: Laser Pointer Frustration
- **Issue:** Laser pointer play can cause frustration in pets unable to complete hunting sequence (catch → kill)
- **Our position:** Primarily affects dogs; cats handle abstract play better
- **Our solution:** Treat dispenser module launches treats at game end, allowing cat to "capture" and "consume" prey equivalent, completing the hunting sequence
- **Mention in research:** Acknowledge the risk, note the differentiation, position treat dispenser as mitigation and competitive advantage

Required Documents and Their Structure
A. Project Charter
This is the initial document to outline your project. It must include:

Business Case: Reasoning for initiating a project or task.

Opportunity Statement: Problem specification.

Goal Statement

Project Scope

Project Stakeholders

Team Selection: MBA Students, their roles, and competences (teams must be 3-4 persons).

Project Plan: Milestones and schedule.

Communication Plan: Tools, meetings.

Project Deliverables: Measurable and tangible outcome of the project.

Project Constraints: Risks, budget, resources.

B. The MBA Capstone Project (The Dissertation)
This is the final written thesis (approx. 20,000 words). It must be written in English and include:

Description & Theory: A description of the project and a theoretical part based on a review of relevant literature.

Comprehensive Business Research & Analysis: * Market/competitors analysis.

Financial analysis.

Quantitative/qualitative stakeholder research.

Risk analysis.

Strategy & Planning: Proposed project schedule and marketing strategy based on your research.

The Prototype: The expected practical outcome (e.g., an application, game, internet platform, company guide, or training package).

Evaluation: An evaluation of the prototype by target users and recommendations for the final version.

C. Executive Summary
A document sent to the Examination Committee members to prepare them for your defence. It must:

Summarize the key points of the Capstone Project.

Restate the purpose of the project.

Highlight the major points, deliverables, and business research findings.

Describe any results, conclusions, or recommendations.

Provide enough information so the reader can understand the full project without reading the entire thesis.

D. Defence Presentation

The final slide deck/presentation you will use during your oral defence.

3. Full Timeline and Necessary Actions
Here is the exact step-by-step sequence of events and deadlines you must follow:

10.01.2026 — Topic & Charter Submission: Submit the final MBA Capstone Project topic and the Project Charter to the MBA Programme Office.

31.01.2026 — Supervisor Assignment: The MBA Director and Quality Standard Leads will assign a supervisor to your project team.

08.05.2026 — Pitching Session: Obligatory participation in the project pitching session. The final draft of your pitch must be agreed upon with your supervisor beforehand.

30.09.2026 — Project Submission to Supervisor: Submit the completed MBA Capstone Project (the full dissertation) to your supervisor.

30.10.2026 — Anti-Plagiarism Check: Submit the MBA Capstone Project in an electronic form to the MBA Office to be checked by the university's anti-plagiarism system. (This is a strict prerequisite for defence approval).

14.11.2026 — Executive Summary Submission: Submit the Executive Summary in electronic form to the MBA Office.

14.11.2026 — Presentation Submission: Submit the final version of the defence examination presentation in electronic form to the MBA Office.

11, 12, or 13.12.2026 — Defence Examination: Attend the final MBA Capstone Project Defence Examination. This is a 45-60 minute oral exam in front of an Examination Committee of 5-6 experts. It will consist of a 15-minute presentation by your team, followed by questions and feedback.

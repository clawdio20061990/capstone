# Reactacat Prototype Testing: Pre-Test Questionnaire

**When to complete:** Before receiving the Reactacat device  
**Estimated time:** 2 minutes

---

## Questions

**1. How many cats do you have?**
- 1 cat
- 2 cats
- 3 or more cats

**2. What is your cat's age? (If multiple cats, indicate each age or average age)**
- Under 1 year (kitten)
- 1–3 years
- 3–7 years
- 7–10 years
- Over 10 years (senior)

**3. How would you describe your cat's personality?**
- Playful
- Lazy
- Shy
- Curious
- Other: _____________

**4. Do you currently play with your cat using any toys? Which ones?**
- (Open text, 1-2 sentences)

**5. On a scale 1-5, how active is your cat daily?**
- 1 (very lazy, sleeps most of the day)
- 2 (somewhat inactive)
- 3 (moderately active)
- 4 (quite active)
- 5 (very active, constantly moving)

**6. Do you use laser toys? If yes, does your cat lose interest over time?**
- Yes, and my cat loses interest quickly
- Yes, and my cat stays interested
- No, I don't use laser toys
- Used to, but stopped because cat lost interest
